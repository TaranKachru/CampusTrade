Rent or Buy Basic Necessities

Can we trade with any campus students?
Trading and renting will only be allowed within the same campus.
How will it be ensured that trading remains within campus?
Each college will have its own email ID system for students. To register, students must use their college email ID, which will grant them access to the trading/renting platform.
What items can I list for rent or sale?
You can list any item for rent or sale, including bicycles, utensils, rooms (if you're leaving for a few days), books, and more.
How will it be ensured that the person returns the rented item on time?
If the person is unable to return the item on time without extending the rental period, they will be charged an additional fee for each day the item is not returned.
What is the payment procedure?
Payment must be made through the website using online payment methods. The item can then be collected from the other person in an in-person transaction.
